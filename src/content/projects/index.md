## Featured Projects

### [Enterprise Data Model](/projects/data-model/)

**Fragmented source systems to unified Silver/Gold model trusted across reporting, ML, and automation.**

**5 source systems unified | 30% faster retrieval | 99% data accuracy**

Unified disconnected source systems into a governed data model used across reporting, ML, and automation workflows. **Databricks, PySpark, SQL**

Category: Data Engineering

### [Semantic Layer & KPI Framework](/projects/semantic-layer/)

**Disputed metrics across teams to single governed KPI layer with consistent definitions.**

**100+ governed DAX measures | 50% faster queries | 15 hrs/week saved**

Standardized KPI logic in one semantic layer to eliminate metric drift and cut report build time. **SSAS, DAX, Power BI**

Category: BI & Analytics

### [Revenue Operations Pipeline & Alerting Platform](/projects/ad-pipeline/)

**Spreadsheet-tracked revenue ops to real-time pipeline with proactive pacing alerts.**

**4 production pipelines | Same-day Slack alerts | 3 teams unified**

Connected ad requests, impressions, pacing, and errors into a monitored event-to-revenue pipeline. **Databricks, Python, Slack, GAM API**

Category: Data Engineering

### [BI Modernization Roadmap](/projects/bi-migration/)

**Fragmented legacy BI to governed semantic-layer reporting across the organization.**

**3 phases, zero disruption | 35% lower maintenance | 40% higher adoption**

Led a phased modernization from legacy tooling to Power BI, then to shared data and semantic-layer reporting. **Power BI, Tableau, Databricks**

Category: BI & Analytics

### [CRM Campaign Automation Platform](/projects/jarvis/)

**Manual campaign creation to automated scenario engine with feedback loops.**

**Millions of profiles/day | 4 recommendation scenarios | Multi-day to daily automation**

Automated scenario creation, scheduled activation, and campaign performance feedback for CRM operations. **Python, CleverTap API, Databricks**

Categories: AI & Automation, Data Engineering

### [Voice-of-Customer Intelligence Platform](/projects/enigma/)

**Unstructured multilingual feedback to searchable insight layer with natural language Q&A.**

**4 social sources unified | Hours to seconds via NL query | 2 Genie spaces + Supervisor Agent**

Structured social and product feedback, then enabled NL query access through Genie and retrieval workflows. **Databricks, NLP, RAG, Genie**

Categories: AI & Automation, Data Science
