# Executive KPI Dashboard Suite

> **Outcome:** Executive review cycles shifted from static reports to interactive drill-down workflows, lifting data accessibility 30% and cutting manual reporting effort 20%.

**Organization**: Jaguar & Land Rover (via Scan Technology LLC)
**Role**: BI Consultant
**Timeline**: February 2017 - May 2017
**Industry**: Automotive
**Ownership**: Primary dashboard developer for design, interaction flow, and business handover

Executive teams needed faster access to KPI performance with the ability to drill into key drivers during decision cycles.

## Challenge

- **Limited drill capability**: Static views did not support deeper analysis during reviews
- **Slow decision loops**: KPI questions required additional follow-up reports
- **Low self-service**: Business users needed analyst support for routine breakdowns

## Approach

- Designed Tableau dashboards optimized for executive KPI monitoring
- Implemented drill-down and filtering interactions for quick root-cause exploration
- Structured views around sales, inventory, and operational performance dimensions
- Delivered user walkthroughs to support adoption in business review sessions

## Results & Impact

- Leadership gained faster access to KPI context during review discussions
- Users could investigate trends directly without waiting for separate extracts
- Reporting conversations shifted from data access to decision action

## Tech Stack

- Tableau
- SQL

## Reusable Pattern

The same executive dashboard approach works across industries:

- **Retail**: Sales, margin, and stock visibility
- **Manufacturing**: Throughput and quality KPI tracking
- **Services**: Operational performance and SLA monitoring
