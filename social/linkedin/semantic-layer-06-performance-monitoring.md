A visual that used to load in two seconds was taking thirty by the time anyone senior saw it. The model hadn't changed. The data hadn't changed. The query had just been getting slower for weeks, with nobody watching.

Slow queries don't break trust in one dramatic moment. They wear it down until the whole dashboard feels unreliable and you can't point at a single cause.

A weekly loop catches it early: review the telemetry, trace each regression back to its layer, give it an owner and a date, then confirm the fix actually moved the original number.
