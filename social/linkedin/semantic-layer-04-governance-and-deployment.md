A Friday-evening deploy was off by a single default setting. By Saturday morning every dashboard was showing zero.

Partitions gone, roles wiped, Sunday lost to a restore, and then a month spent earning back trust the deploy had burned in about thirty seconds.

The answer isn't more careful engineers, it's gates. Owner sign-off with a real change summary. A validation pass: regression on the measures, role checks, smoke tests. And a rollback you wrote down before the deploy, not one you improvise in the middle of the incident.
