A booked campaign finished a Friday under its commitment. The shortfall had been on screen since Wednesday, in a pacing spreadsheet someone rebuilt by hand every morning, and the morning it mattered they were buried in the booking inbox.

By Friday it was make-goods, pulled from inventory already promised to another campaign, so the miss just rolled forward.

Reactive ad ops isn't a people problem, it's a missing pipeline. Four signals turn it into a loop you can actually act on: inventory, impressions, pacing, and VAST errors. Alert on business impact instead of noise, and flag content ops before a commitment slips.
