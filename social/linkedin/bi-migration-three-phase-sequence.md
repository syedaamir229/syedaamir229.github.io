Halfway through a platform migration, the same KPI came back three percent apart on the old and new tools. The sponsor wanted to know which number to put in front of the board.

Nobody could say. The tool, the data layer, and a hundred metric definitions had all moved at once, so there was no way to point at the layer that caused the gap. Leadership did the rational thing and went back to the operations spreadsheet, and a project meant to consolidate trust ended up scattering it.

The fix is patience. Move the tool, then the data layer, then the metric logic. One variable at a time. When a number shifts, and it will, you know exactly what moved it.
